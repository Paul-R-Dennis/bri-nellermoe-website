Secrets in Embers

A kingdom kept in ash. A court built on beautiful lies. And a love that threatens to burn everything they were told to protect.

Gaisha was once a vast and diverse realm, beloved and cherished by all who shared her. Until war ravaged the once thriving home…and she fell. Split into seven, her history was nearly erased save for the few who remember.

A thousand years later, Eve Calloway has spent her entire life living with the secret of being a witch — and plagued by nightmares that grow more vivid, more lucid, more tangible than ever before.
