Embers in Time · Book One

Secrets in Embers

A kingdom kept in ash. A court built on beautiful lies. And a love that threatens to burn everything they were told to protect.

Gaisha was once a vast and diverse realm, beloved and cherished by all who shared her. Until war ravaged the once thriving home…and she fell. Split into seven, her history was nearly erased save for the few who remember. The few who hold on.

Centuries later, deep in the ancient forests of Calatheas, the two unlikeliest of souls—she of the Witches and he of the Fae—found one another. Despite his family hunting hers for power, they fell in love and dreamed to reunite their home, once and for all. That is, until a long-ago promise was broken…
