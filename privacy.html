Privacy Policy

How your information is handled here

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit brinellermoe.com (the “Site”), how it is used, and the choices you have. The Site is operated by Bri Nellermoe.

Information collected

The Site collects personal information only when you choose to provide it, and a limited amount of anonymous usage data automatically:

  • Email address — when you sign up for the mailing list or submit the contact form.
  • Contact form details — any name or message you include when you reach out.
  • Usage data — anonymized information such as pages visited, approximate location, device, and browser, collected automatically through analytics cookies.

How your information is used

  • To send newsletters, release news, and related updates you have subscribed to.
  • To respond to messages you send through the contact form.
  • To understand how visitors use the Site so it can be improved.

Your information is never sold or rented to third parties.

Third-party services

The Site relies on a small number of trusted services that may process your information on its behalf:

  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — manages the mailing list and sends newsletters. See Kit's privacy policy.
  • Web3Forms — delivers contact form submissions. See Web3Forms' privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics — provides anonymized traffic statistics. See Google's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the Site. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Cookies

The Site uses cookies for analytics to measure traffic and understand how the Site is used. Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to read the Site or sign up for the mailing list.

Your choices and rights

  • You can unsubscribe from the mailing list at any time using the link at the bottom of any email.
  • You can request a copy of the personal information held about you, or ask for it to be corrected or deleted, by getting in touch.

Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and information is not knowingly collected from them.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. When it changes, the “Last updated” date above will be revised.

Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, email brinellermoeauthor@gmail.com.
